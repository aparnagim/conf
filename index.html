About the LEARN Meeting

The LEARN Meeting is an annual gathering of Sri Lanka’s research and education community. In 2025, we launch two new Working Groups—GenAI Empowerment and AgriTech—to drive collaborative projects and capacity-building across our member institutions. The two-day program also includes the Annual General Meeting (AGM), the LEARN Board of Directors Meeting, the Opening Ceremony of the LEARN meetings, and a Services Awareness Session.

Who should attend

  • VCs, Registrars, Bursars & official delegates (AGM)
  • Academics, researchers, technology leaders & administrators (WGs)
  • Campus IT, library, e-learning teams; prospective members (Services)

Highlights

  • Launch two national WGs (roadmaps + coordinators)
  • Member governance at the 16th AGM
  • Deep-dive on LEARN services (eduID, eduroam, eduVPN, CSIRT, TAC)
  • Opening Ceremony & Social Dinner

Working Groups

WG 1: AgriTech

Connect agriculture faculties, research centres, and IT teams to apply digital technologies—IoT, remote sensing, data platforms, AI—across education, research, and extension.

  • 2–3 collaborative pilots (e.g., smart farm sensors; data repository)
  • Quarterly knowledge-sharing sessions + community portal
  • Roadmap with roles, milestones, and metrics

WG 2: GenAI Empowerment

Build national capacity to use Generative AI responsibly in teaching, research, student services, and NREN operations.

  • 2–3 pilot projects + 2–3 training programs
  • Practical guidelines for safe, ethical GenAI use
  • Elected coordinator; regular meetups
